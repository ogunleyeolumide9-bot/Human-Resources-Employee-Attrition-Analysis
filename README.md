## Overview

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations, impacting productivity, employee morale, and operational costs. This project analyzes an HR Employee Attrition dataset to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover and provide data-driven recommendations to improve employee retention.

This repository documents the dataset and provides a foundation, performed data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, and dashboard development to uncover meaningful insights that support strategic HR decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

High employee turnover can lead to increased recruitment costs, loss of experienced talent, and reduced organizational performance. The Human Resources department needs to understand the primary causes of employee attrition and identify employee groups that are at higher risk of leaving.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition trends across departments and job roles.
* Measure the organization's overall attrition rate.
* Examine the relationship between overtime and employee turnover.
* Identify demographic groups with the highest attrition.
* Evaluate the impact of job satisfaction, income, and years at the company on employee retention.
* Develop an interactive dashboard to support HR decision-making.

## Key Insights

* Certain departments experience significantly higher employee turnover than others.
* Employees who work overtime are more likely to leave the organization.
* Employees with lower job satisfaction show a higher attrition rate.
* Younger employees represent a larger proportion of employee exits.
* Lower monthly income appears to be associated with increased employee turnover.
* Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to resign.

---

## Recommendations

* Develop retention strategies for departments with high attrition.
* Reduce excessive overtime to improve employee well-being.
* Review compensation packages for lower-income job roles.
* Invest in employee engagement and career development programs.
* Conduct regular employee satisfaction surveys.
* Implement targeted retention initiatives for new employees.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can help Human Resources teams understand employee turnover patterns and make informed decisions that improve workforce retention.
